About The Show

Welcome to Good Playing With You

Good Playing With You is your weekly gaming hangout — sharp, funny, and genuinely passionate about video games. Every Friday, Dan Whitehill and Matt Lord break down the biggest gaming news, hottest releases, juiciest leaks, and the stories the industry doesn't want you to miss.

Whether you're a casual player who picks up a controller on weekends or a hardcore enthusiast who watches every Nintendo Direct live — this show is for you. With straight, LGBTQ+, and more representation on the crew, Good Playing With You is a place for everyone who loves games.

New episodes drop every Friday on YouTube, Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and everywhere else you listen.

The Crew

Dan Whitehill

Co-Host & Executive Producer

Dan Whitehill is a Los Angeles-based digital media executive and lifelong gamer. Over a decade at NBCUniversal's E! News, he built and led a digital video operation that generated over one billion video views, produced more than 150 videos per week across four YouTube channels, drove $2M+ in YouTube revenue in a single year, and launched a FAST channel on Peacock. He also built a custom CMS and cloud encoding workflow from scratch — because that's what happens when you combine a media career with a serious tech obsession.

Dan's love of gaming started with Missile Command on the Atari and a stint at G4TV. Today he's equally at home on Xbox Series X, PlayStation 5, and Switch — always hunting for the next great game. Super Mario Bros. 3 is his all-time favorite, though he's a die-hard fan of all things Zelda. When he's not recording, he's hiking with his two dogs, Jax and Woody, building his clothing brand Instant Riot, or getting sniped in Fortnite.

Matt Lord

Co-Host

Matt Lord is a media professional from Thousand Oaks, CA and a lifelong gamer who brings encyclopedic gaming knowledge and sharp instincts to every episode. He's the guy who somehow has a hot take ready before the news even finishes breaking — and he's usually right.

Matt loves the full spectrum of gaming — from the quiet intensity of single-player adventures to the chaos of multiplayer matches. Death Stranding and Overwatch are his go-to games, but he's always ready to argue passionately about whatever just came out. Whether on console or PC, Matt brings real-friend energy and honest reactions that keep Good Playing With You grounded in genuine gamer culture.

Zach Kohler

Intern Extraordinaire

Zach Kohler is the unsung hero behind the scenes at Good Playing With You. As our resident Intern Extraordinaire, Zach handles the behind-the-scenes publishing, writing, and content production that keeps this show running week after week.

A gamer at heart with a sharp editorial eye, Zach brings fresh perspective and tireless energy to the GPWY operation. He's proof that the best content teams aren't just about what happens in front of the mic — and we couldn't do it without him.
