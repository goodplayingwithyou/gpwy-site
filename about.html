About The Guys

Welcome to Good Playing With You

Join Dan Whitehill and Matt Lord — the ultimate destination for gamers who live, breathe, and dream video games.

Whether you're a casual player or a hardcore enthusiast, Good Playing With You dives deep into the latest game releases, industry news, and epic gaming moments you can't afford to miss.

With straight, LGBTQ+, and more representation on the crew, this show is for everyone who loves games. New episodes drop every Friday — subscribe wherever you listen.

Meet The Hosts

Dan Whitehill

Co-Host & Producer

Dan Whitehill is a Los Angeles-based digital media executive and lifelong gamer. With over a decade of experience leading digital video operations at NBCUniversal's E! News — where he managed four YouTube channels, generated over one billion video views, and launched a FAST channel on Peacock — Dan brings serious media expertise to every episode.

When he's not breaking down the week's biggest gaming headlines, Dan is deep in whatever RPG currently has his attention, building his clothing brand Instant Riot, or nerding out about content strategy and technology.

Matt Lord

Co-Host

Matt Lord is the sharp, funny voice that keeps Good Playing With You grounded in real gamer culture. With an encyclopedic knowledge of gaming history and an instinct for the perfect hot take, Matt makes every episode feel like you're hanging out with your most knowledgeable gaming friend.

Matt brings real-friend energy, honest reactions, and a genuine passion for games across every platform — from PlayStation to Nintendo to PC. He's the reason you'll keep coming back every Friday.

Your weekly gaming hangout. Sharp takes on news, reviews, and industry chaos — every Friday.
